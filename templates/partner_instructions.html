Usage Instructions


The partner app main page is divided into two panels:

1) The information panel


The top half of the information panel contains: the current assignment number, the date by which a partner must be chosen, the date that partner evaluations open, and the date by which a partner evaluation must be submitted.

The top half of information panel also displays a number of different notifications:

a) Current Partner Notifications:

This info box will either tell you that you don't have a partner, the name of your current partner (if you have one), or that you were given permission to work alone for the current assignment.

b) Invitation Notification:

This info box will inform you if you've received new partner invitations for the current assignment.

c) Dropped Partner Notification:

This info box will inform you if your partner for the current assignment accepted an invitation from another student and dissovled your partnership. If you see this notification, it is your responsibility to find a new partner, reinstate your dissolved partnership, or contact your TA to determine an appropriate course of action for the current assignment.

The bottom half of the information panel contains a history of your confirmed partnerships. If the history is empty, you haven't been involved in any confirmed partnerships.

2) The action panel


The action panel contains three buttons:

a) Send Invitations

This button brings up a window that allows you to send parntership invitations to students in your lab sections. By default, the app does not allow you to send invitations to students with whom you're currently partnered, or with whom you've had a partnership. If you think you need to work alone or repeat a partnership, please contact your TA. Only TAs can confirm NO PARTNER partnerships or repeated partnerships.

b) Received Invitations

This button allows you to confirm invitations that you've received from other students. Only after an invitation is confirmed will a partnership be considered active. Once an invitation is confirmed, all other invitations involving you (all invitations you've sent and received) and the student who sent the invitation will be deactivated.

c) Partner Evaluation

This button allows you to fill out an evaluation for your partner for the current assignment. The evaluation form will only be available after the partner selection period closes. If you worked alone--with or without the permission of a TA--you will not be able to submit an evaluation.

The bottom half of the action panel displays that invitations that you've sent that are currently active.
