Usage Instructions


The partner app main page is divided into various UI components. I'll list them here as they appear from top to bottom on the main partner landing page (the page you see by default after successfully logging into the partner app):

1) The Navigation Bar

Once logged in, the navigation bar contains 6 buttons (1 of them is a dropdown). From left to right, they are:

a) The Partner App Button

If logged in, this button will take you to the main partner app page (the page that you first see when you log in).

b) The History Dropdown

The history dropdown contains two buttons:

i) The Partner/Eval History Button

This button takes you to a page that displays three panels.

  • The Abbreviated Partner History panel This panel displays your partner history as your TAs see it.
  • The Complete Partner History panel This panel displays your complete partner history, including any inactive (cancelled) partnerships you've had.
  • The Eval History panel This panel displays all the partner evaluations you've submitted, including inactive evaluations.
ii) The Invitation History Button

This button takes you to a page that displays all invitations you've ever sent/received. If you know you've sent/received an invitation, but you don't see it on this page, please report it to your TA.

c) The Profile Button

This button takes you to a page in which you can add information about yourself to your profile. Other students will be able to see this information on the Browse Partners page.

d) The Browse Partners Button

This button takes you to the Browse Partners page. Here, you can see the profiles of and send invitations to all students who don't have partners for the current assignment (if there is more than one active assignment, the current assignment is the one with the later due date).

e) The Help Button

If you're reading this, you already know where the help button takes you!

f) The Logout Button

The logout button logs you out of the partner app. Note: Logging our of the partner app will also log you out of other Google services that you're logged into.

2) The Notification Area

The notification area is the white space directly below the nav bar (where you see the "Hello, [student]" greeting). Whenever something needs your attention, you'll see a notification banner under the greeting. These banners will call your attention to things like partner invitations you've received, an unused student profile, and any cancelled partnerships you were involved in.

3) The Action Panel

The action panel contains three buttons. Left to right, they are:

a) The Send Partner Invitations Button

You'll only see this button when there's an assignment with an active partner selection period. If you can't see this button: it's either too late or too early to pick a partner for a particular assignment.

Clicking on this button will cause the partner invitation dialog box to appear. In this dialog, you can send invitations to students for specfic assignments. The partner selection dialog contains a form with two input fields:

  • Assignment A dropdown that contains a list of active assignments. If there are multiple active assignments, make sure you choose the correct assignment.
  • Partner A dropdown that contains a list of students that you can send invitations to. Depending on the rules for your course, this dropdown may contain every student in your class, or, in most cases, just the students in your lab section.

b) The View Invitations from Others Button

Like the Send Partner Invitations button, you'll only see this button if there's an assignment with an active partner selection period.

Clicking on this button will cause the received invitation dialog box to appear. This dialog contains a single dropdown that contains the invitations you've received that are currently active. You can confirm an invitation by choosing, from the dropdown, the name of the student who sent you the invitation (make sure the invitation has the correct assignment number), and clicking the "Submit" button.

c) The Partner Evaluation Button

You'll only see the partner evalution button if there is an assignment with an active partner evaluation period.

Clicking on this button will cause the partner evaluation dialog box to appear. This dialog contains a partner evaluation form with various questions regarding your partner and your lab assignment. Note: you can submit evaluations for the same partner/assignment multiple times. The only evaluation that will be used is the last one you submit.

If you didn't successfully register a partnership for an assignment, you won't be able to submit an evalution for that assignment. If you believe you registered a partnership but are unable to submit and evaluation, please contact your TA.

4) The Assignment Panel

The assignments panel contains two tables that give you infomation about the assignments that have open partner selection/partner evalution periods. From left to right:

a) The Assignments Open for Partner Selection Table

This table shows information about assingments that have open partner selection periods. This table shows you--from left to right--the assignment's number, the date/time when you need to have a partner for this assingment, the date/time when partner evaluations open for this assignment, and whether or not you have a partner for this assignment. If you thought you or your partner confirmed an invitation for an assignment, but this table shows that you don't have a partner, please contact your TA.

b) The Assignments Open for Partner Evalution Table

This table shows information about assingments that have open partner evalution periods. This table shows you--from left to right--the assignment's number, the date/time when partner evaluations are due, and whether you've submitted an evalution for this assignment. Partner evaluations cannot be reopened for students who miss deadlines, so please make sure you submit your evaluations in a timely manner.

5) The Invitation Panel

The invitation panel contains two tables that give you information about the open invitations that you've send/received. Note: your invitations (send and received) are deactivated (closed) when you enter into a partnership. From left to right:

The Open Invitations You've Sent to Others Table

This table contains information about open invitations that you've sent to others.

The Invitations Others Have Sent to You Table

This table contains information about open invitations that you've received from others.

6) The Cancelled Partnerships Panel

You'll only be able to see this panel if there is an assignment with and active partner selection period for which you have cancelled partnerships. A partnership can only be cancelled if you/your partner enters into a partnership (for the same assignment) with another student. Please contact your TA if you have unexpected cancelled partnerships.
